Live sessions

Pre-recorded talks raise needs, questions and suggestions for standardization work, captured as GitHub issues.

Three live sessions are scheduled during the week of 15-19 November 2021 to review these issues and converge on a common understanding of whether and how they may progress from a standardization perspective.

The calls are open to all workshop participants. If you did not receive calendar invites with meeting information, please contact François Daoust <fd@w3.org>.

Familiarity with workshop talks and issues is recommended. There will be no speakers and presentations during these calls. We will rather ask participants to take an active role and help shape next steps for each of the topics.

When: Monday 15 November - 4pm UTC - 90mn (see local time conversion)

Summary of issues:

  • Web Audio
  • Media synchronization
